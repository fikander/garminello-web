Trello boards on Garmin devices

Garminello allows you to view your Trello boards and lists. All boards visible from you Trello account will be available in the app.

To set up, simply

  • Create and account on this site.
  • Link your account to Trello from your profile page.
  • Install and launch Garmin app. It will display an 8 character activation code.
  • Use this code to register your Garmin device from your profile page.

Play sequences defined in Trello (e.g. workouts)

To play any Trello list, choose relevant board in the app, navigate to the list and choose [Play List] from the menu (see the app diagram below).

To define how long each card from the list will show for, simply add duration to the Trello card summary, e.g. "Warm up [3m 30s]"

Format the duration as minutes and seconds in the following way: [XXm YYs]. You don't have to specify both minutes and seconds. Examples:

  • "Push-ups [2m 30s]"
  • "Rest [30s]"
  • "Meditation [15m]".

While playing the list, each card will be shown for the defined duration.
